Honda Vietnam hien duy tri cam ket dau tu dai han tai thi truong noi dia voi tong nguon von dat tren 112,8 trieu USD. Trong giai doan tu nam 1999 den nam 2003, cac dong xe chu luc nhu Super Dream, Future va Wave Alpha lien tuc nhan duoc su tin nhiem tu nguoi tieu dung thong qua danh hieu Hang Viet Nam chat luong cao. De nang cao nang luc thich ung voi thi truong, vao ngay 22 thang 8 nam 2003, Trung tam Nghien cuu va Phat trien thu 13 cua Honda tren toan cau da chinh thuc di vao hoat dong tai Viet Nam. Buoc di chien luoc nay nham toi uu hoa viec nam bat thi hieu va nhu cau cua khach hang ban dia mot cach nhanh chong va chinh xac nhat.

He thong phan phoi cua doanh nghiep hien bao phu toan quoc voi mang luoi 167 cua hang ban xe va dich vu do Honda uy nhiem, trien khai dong bo mo hinh dich vu 4S bao gom ban hang, bao hanh, phu tung chinh hang va huong dan lai xe an toan. Ben canh do, ty le noi dia hoa cua cac san pham cung ghi nhan nhung con so an tuong khi Super Dream dat 76,04 phan tram, Future dat 70,18 phan tram va Wave Alpha dat 79,31 phan tram. Su ho tro tu 41 nha cung cap phu tung trong nuoc voi cong nghe san xuat hien dai da giup duy tri tieu chuan chat luong toan cau cho moi san pham.

Hoat dong xuat khau cung chung kien su tang truong manh me khi tinh den het nam 2003, doanh nghiep da xuat di hon 34.000 xe Wave Alpha, rieng nam 2003 chiem hon 26.000 chiec. Linh vuc xuat khau phu tung cung bat dau ghi dau an voi 302.460 chi tiet duoc xuat sang cac thi truong khu vuc, trong do Thai Lan tieu thu 111.600 chi tiet va Malaysia tieu thu 190.860 chi tiet, khang dinh vi the cua Honda Viet Nam trong chuoi cung ung quoc te.
