He thong dang gap phai tinh trang loi illegal operation lien tuc khi van hanh cac ung dung nhu Microsoft Word hay JetAudio. Cac thong bao loi thuong chi ra loi truy cap bo nho khong hop le trong module Kernel32.dll tai cac dia chi bo nho cu the, gay ra hien tuong treo may va buoc nguoi dung phai can thiep bang Task Manager hoac khoi dong lai phan cung. Nguyen nhan co the den tu viec xung dot phan mem moi cai dat hoac su co nghiem trong trong viec quan ly tai nguyen cua he dieu hanh.

Kernel32.dll dong vai tro la mot tep tin thu vien lien ket dong quan trong bac nhat, chiu trach nhiem dieu phoi toan bo bo nho RAM, bo nho ao cung nhu cac thao tac nhap xuat giua phan mem va phan cung. Khi khoi dong, tep tin nay duoc nap vao vung bo nho duoc bao ve de giam sat moi hoat dong cua nguoi dung. Bat ky su co nao xay ra voi module nay deu khien cac tien trinh ung dung bi gian doan do khong the truy xuat tai nguyen he thong mot cach chinh xac.

De khac phuc, nguoi dung can kiem tra lai thong so phan cung nhu dung luong RAM va o cung con trong. Cac cong cu bao tri he thong nhu Disk Cleanup, Scan Disk va Disk Defragmenter nen duoc su dung de toi uu hoa cau truc du lieu tren thiet bi luu tru. Mac du co the can thiep vao Registry hoac su dung cac phan mem sua loi DLL chuyen dung de xu ly triet de cac phan vung loi, nhung day la nhung thao tac ky thuat phuc tap doi hoi su can than cao. Trong truong hop cac bien phap bao tri thong thuong khong mang lai hieu qua, viec cai dat lai toan bo he dieu hanh Windows la phuong an cuoi cung de thiet lap lai moi truong lam viec on dinh.
