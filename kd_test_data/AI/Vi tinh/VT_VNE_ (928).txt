Magic Ball duoc xem la mot ban nang cap toan dien va xuat sac dua tren nen tang cua tua game huyen thoai Arkanoid. Ve mat cong nghe, tro choi nay da thuc hien mot buoc nhay vot khi chuyen doi toan bo giao dien sang moi truong do hoa 3D hien dai, mang lai nhung hieu ung thi giac va ky xao dac biet ma cac phien ban tien nhiem chua tung co. Co che van hanh co ban van giu nguyen viec su dung be phong de dieu khien trai cau tieu diet cac vat can, tuy nhien nha phat trien da tich hop them nhieu tinh nang chien dau moi nhu he thong rocket va dan chum de tang tinh hap dan. Cac kieu tuong chan cung duoc thiet ke lai voi cau truc phuc tap hon, doi hoi nguoi choi phai co su tinh toan ve goc do va toc do khi tuong tac voi moi truong vat ly trong game.

De co the van hanh on dinh ung dung nay, he thong phan cung cua nguoi dung can dap ung cac tieu chuan ky thuat co ban bao gom bo vi xu ly Pentium 300MHz va bo nho trong RAM toi thieu 64MB. Ve phan mem, tro choi tuong thich tot tren cac nen tang Windows 2000, Windows ME va Windows XP, dong thoi yeu cau thu vien do hoa DirectX 3.0 cung card do hoa co kha nang xu ly 3D chuyen dung. Day la mot yeu cau cau hinh kha de chiu va phu hop voi phan lon cac may tinh ca nhan tai thoi diem hien tai. Neu muon kiem chung hieu nang thuc te cung nhu chi tiet do hoa cua Magic Ball, nguoi dung co the tai ve ban dung thu co dung luong rat gon nhe chi khoang 6MB de trai nghiem nhanh cac tinh nang dot pha truoc khi quyet dinh cai dat phien ban day du.
