He thong may tinh su dung bo mach chu Gigabyte 915P-D Pro cung vi xu ly Intel 3.2GHz socket LGA775 dang gap van de nghiem trong ve tieng on phat ra tu he thong tan nhiet. Mac du cau hinh di kem nguon 450W va bo nho DDR2 nhung quat tan nhiet stock cua Intel luon van hanh o toc do vong quay RPM rat cao ngay ca khi khong tai hoac trong qua trinh khoi dong. Nguyen nhan chu yeu khong nam o loi co khi ma do dac tinh toa nhiet lon cua dong chip nay khien cam bien dieu khien quat phai tang toc de duy tri nhiet do an toan. Dac biet socket 775 co co che thiet ke chan cam khac biet hoan toan so voi socket 478 nen cac giai phap tan nhiet cu khong the tai su dung.

De khac phuc tinh trang nay nguoi dung nen can nhac thay the bang cac bo tan nhiet roi tu nhung thuong hieu nhu Cooler Master hoac Zalman voi thiet ke canh quat lon va be mat tiep xuc bang dong tot hon. Cac giai phap nhu tan nhiet bang chat long hay da kho hien van chua thuc su pho bien va kho tiep can tai thi truong noi dia. Ben canh do viec su dung phan mem nhu Speedfan hoac Motherboard Monitor 5 co the giup theo doi va dieu chinh fan curve mot cach chu dong. Mot thu thuat ky thuat khac la dieu chinh dien ap quat xuong muc 7V thay vi 12V mac dinh de giam tieng on trong khi van dam bao luu luong gio on dinh.

Ngoai ra tieng on tong the cua thung may con co the den tu quat nguon hoac quat chipset tren mainboard. Viec toi uu hoa luu thong khi trong thung may bang cach lap them quat case chay o dien ap thap se giam nhiet do moi truong ben trong tu do giam tai cho quat CPU. Nguoi dung cung can kiem tra do rung cua vo may va do chat che khi lap dat linh kien de han che cong huong am thanh. Neu dieu kien cho phep viec dau tu vao cac linh kien chuyen dung cho silent PC nhu nguon Seasonic hoac vo case cach am se mang lai hieu qua cach am triet de nhat cho he thong.
