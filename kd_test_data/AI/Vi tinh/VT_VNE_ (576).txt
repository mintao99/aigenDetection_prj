Ma doc Wallon duoc ghi nhan lan dau tai khu vuc chau Au vao trung tuan thang 5 voi muc tieu tan cong truc dien vao trinh phat da phuong tien Windows Media Player cua Microsoft. Khac voi cac dong virus phat tan qua tep dinh kem truyen thong nhu NetSky hay Bagle, tac gia cua Wallon da thay doi chien thuat sang su dung cac lien ket nhung trong noi dung email de danh lua nguoi dung. Khi truy cap vao duong dan nay, ma doc se tu dong duoc tai ve va xam nhap vao cac he thong chay Windows tu phien ban 95 den XP. Co che hoat dong cua Wallon kha tinh vi khi no duy tri trang thai an minh cho den khi nguoi dung khoi chay cac dinh dang tep tin MP3 hoac video bang trinh phat mac dinh tren may tinh.

Ve mat ky thuat, Wallon khong so huu toc do lay lan nhanh do yeu cau su tuong tac truc tiep tu phia nguoi dung, nhung muc do gay hai lai rat nghiem trong. Sau khi duoc kich hoat, ma doc se tien hanh ghi de va thay the tep tin thuc thi wmplayer.exe, khien cong cu nghe nhin nay hoan toan bi vo hieu hoa. Dong thoi, no tu dong gui cac email dinh dang HTML chua lien ket doc hai den danh sach lien lac trong so dia chi cua nan nhan de tiep tuc vong doi phat tan. De khac phuc su co, cac chuyen gia khuyen nghi nguoi dung can trien khai ban cap nhat bao mat MS04-13 va thuc hien quet he thong bang cac cong cu chuyen dung nhu Trend Micro. Trong truong hop tep thuc thi da bi hong, viec cai dat lai Windows Media Player tu nguon dia goc hoac trang chu Microsoft la bat buoc sau khi da lam sach moi truong he dieu hanh. De dam bao an toan thong tin, nguoi dung nen hinh thanh thoi quen truy cap website bang cach nhap dia chi truc tiep vao thanh duyet trinh thay vi nhap vao cac lien ket la trong thu dien tu.
