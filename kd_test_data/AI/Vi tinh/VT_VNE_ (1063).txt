Microsoft da trien khai mot lop bao mat moi tich hop trong tua game Halo 2 nham loai bo cac thiet bi da qua chinh sua phan cung khoi he thong Xbox Live. Trong nhung ngay qua, cong dong nguoi dung ghi nhan hang loat truong hop bi ngat ket noi vinh vien sau khi truy cap dich vu bang cac may choi game co gan modchip. Dai dien Microsoft khang dinh viec that chat quan ly la can thiet de duy tri mot moi truong giai tri truc tuyen cong bang va thong nhat. Hang nhan manh rang moi hinh thuc can thiep vao cau truc he thong deu vi pham quy dinh va gay anh huong truc tiep den trai nghiem cua nhung nguoi choi chan chinh.

Ve mat ky thuat, modchip la cac bo mach phu tro duoc thiet ke de bypass phan mem he thong, cho phep thuc thi ma nguon tu che, game sao chep hoac tham chi la cac he dieu hanh thay the nhu Linux. Viec nang cap o cung dung luong lon va cai dat cac trinh quan ly da phuong tien da bien Xbox thanh mot thiet bi giai tri da nang nhung cung mo duong cho cac hinh thuc gian lan truc tuyen. Cac chuyen gia phan tich nhan dinh rang Microsoft dang no luc bao ve mo hinh he sinh thai khep kin cua minh. Moi de doa lon nhat khong chi la nan vi pham ban quyen ma con la kha nang can thiep vao du lieu de tao loi the bat hop ly trong cac tran dau mang.

Mot so nguoi dung am hieu cong nghe suy doan rang Microsoft da thuc hien viec quet va doi chieu so seri cua o cung voi co so du lieu he thong ngay khi khoi dong Halo 2. Neu phat hien bat ky sai lech nao so voi thong so goc, quyen truy cap Xbox Live se bi thu hoi ngay lap tuc. Mac du nhieu nguoi da co gang can thiep vao bang mach de vuot qua hang rao nay nhung van gap phai su truy quet gat gao tu phia nha san xuat. Cuoc chien giua cac giao thuc bao mat phan cung va cong dong modding van dang tiep dien khi nguoi dung tin rang moi rao can ky thuat deu co the bi khuat phuc boi cac giai phap ma nguon mo trong tuong lai.
