Google Page Creator dai dien cho mot buoc tien trong viec don gian hoa quy trinh phat trien web bang cach loai bo yeu cau ve kien thuc ngon ngu lap trinh nhu HTML hay Javascript. Cong cu nay cho phep nguoi dung thiet ke giao dien truc quan thong qua viec sap xep cac thanh phan du lieu nhu van ban va hinh anh tren nen tang dien toan dam may cua Google. Kha nang trien khai chi voi mot thao tac nhap chuot giup toi uu hoa viec hien thi noi dung tren da nen tang va moi he dieu hanh khac nhau ma khong gap tro ngai ve mat ky thuat.

De kich hoat dich vu, nguoi dung can su dung tai khoan Google hoac Gmail de xac thuc danh tinh va thiet lap ten mien con theo cau truc mac dinh la googlepages.com. Ve thong so luu tru, Google cung cap han muc dung luong len den 100 MB cho moi tai khoan, mot con so duoc danh gia la du thua doi voi nhu cau thiet ke web co ban. Ben canh do, he thong cung tich hop cac co che kiem duyet noi dung de ngan chan cac du lieu vi pham tieu chuan cong dong, dac biet la noi dung khieu dam, mac du thuat toan loc chi tiet van chua duoc cong bo rong rai.

Khac voi mo hinh Blog von uu tien tinh cap nhat lien tuc, Google Page Creator tap trung vao phan khuc xuat ban noi dung tinh va it bien dong. Hien tai, san pham van dang trong giai doan thu nghiem Beta han che ke tu ngay 23 thang 2. Tai thi truong Viet Nam, nguoi dung can dang ky va cho doi thu xac nhan tu he thong truoc khi co the trai nghiem thuc te do so luong tai khoan thu nghiem van con rat it. Day la thoi diem thich hop de nguoi dung cong nghe dang ky som nham tiep can cong nghe thiet ke web truc tuyen moi nay.
