Spyware dang tro thanh mot thach thuc an ninh mang nghiem trong khi hoat dong nhu mot he thong giam sat ngam trong moi truong may tinh. Nghien cuu thong ke tu Webroot va Earthlink tren quy mo 1,5 trieu thiet bi dau cuoi cho thay trung binh moi PC dang phai doi mat voi 28 tac nhan doc hai. He sinh thai ma doc nay rat da dang, tu adware, cookies theo doi den cac loai Trojan phuc tap duoc lap trinh de trich xuat du lieu hanh vi va thong tin xac thuc. Nhung bien the nguy hiem nhat thuong do cac nhom toi pham mang chuyen nghiep phat trien nham chiem quyen dieu khien he thong de phat tan thu rac hoac danh cap mat khau. Mac du nhieu loai spyware hoat dong am tham, mot so khac lai gay ra cac hien tuong nhu thay doi cau hinh trinh duyet hoac tu dong kich hoat cac cua so quang cao pop up bat chap cac lop phong ve.

Theo bao cao tu don vi kiem soat Icstis, su xuat hien cua dong Trojan dialler dang gay ra ton that kinh te lon do tu dong ket noi den cac dich vu vien thong gia cao. Hinh thuc lay nhiem hien nay da chuyen dich sang phuong thuc drive-by download va tan dung sai sot khi nhap URL cua nguoi dung. Tham chi, viec su dung bang duyet truoc trong Microsoft Outlook cung co the tro thanh vector tan cong cho ma doc ma khong can truy cap website. Spyware hien dai con the hien kha nang chia se thong tin nan nhan giua cac may chu dieu khien va can thiep sau vao cau truc registry cua Windows khien viec go bo thu cong rat kho khan. Cac giai phap bao mat nhu Spy Sweeper hay Ad Aware chi co the phat huy hieu qua neu duoc cap nhat database lien tuc. Ben canh giai phap ky thuat, cac nha lap phap cung dang thuc day du luat moi nham bat buoc su xac nhan cua nguoi dung truoc khi trien khai bat ky tien trinh theo doi nao tren he thong may tinh.
