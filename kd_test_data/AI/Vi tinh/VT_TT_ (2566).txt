Flash Player Pro 2.6 duoc danh gia la mot giai phap phan mem chuyen dung cho phep nguoi dung tuong tac sau voi cac tep tin dinh dang SWF thong qua nhieu tinh nang ky thuat tien ich. Ben canh chuc nang trinh chieu du lieu Flash tieu chuan, ung dung nay con ho tro kha nang trich xuat khung hinh de chuyen doi thanh dinh dang anh BMP chat luong cao. Mot diem nhan cong nghe dang chu y cua phan mem la kha nang can thiep vao he thong de thiet lap cac file Flash lam hinh nen dong hoac chuyen doi chung sang dinh dang SCR de tao trinh bao ve man hinh. Hon the nua, Flash Player Pro con ho tro dong goi cac noi dung Flash thanh tep tin thuc thi EXE doc lap, giup nguoi dung de dang khoi chay truc tiep tren moi truong Windows ma khong can phu thuoc vao cac trinh duyet web hay trinh cam bien ho tro ben thu ba.

He thong dieu khien cua ung dung duoc thiet ke toi uu nhu mot trinh phat media chuyen nghiep voi day du cac tuy chon dieu huong nhu phong to, thu nho, tua nhanh hoac tam dung noi dung dang phat. Nguoi dung co the linh hoat chuyen doi giua che do hien thi toan man hinh va kich thuoc goc cua tep tin, dong thoi thuc hien cac tac vu quan ly du lieu nhu sao chep, di chuyen, xoa hoac thiet lap danh sach yeu thich ngay tren giao dien chuong trinh. Voi bo cai dat gon nhe chi khoang 1,5 MB, phan mem nay mang lai trai nghiem su dung muot ma va de tiep can cho moi doi tuong. Hien tai, nha phat trien Micromedia dang cung cap phien ban Flash Player Pro 2.6 voi muc gia thuong mai la 30 USD, di kem voi do la chinh sach cho phep nguoi dung trai nghiem mien phi toan bo tinh nang trong thoi gian 15 ngay truoc khi quyet dinh thanh toan ban quyen.
