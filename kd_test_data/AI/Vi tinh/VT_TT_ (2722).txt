Google Suggest hien dang la mot giai phap thu nghiem mang tinh dot pha cua Google nham toi uu hoa hieu suat truy van thong tin cho nguoi dung. Ve ban chat day la mot cong cu du doan tu khoa dua tren viec phan tich co so du lieu khong lo tu cac hanh vi tim kiem truoc do cua cong dong. Bang cach cung cap cac goi y thoi gian thuc he thong giup nguoi dung de dang tiep can voi nhung cau tra loi cho cac truy van phuc tap hoac dac thu ma khong can phai go toan bo cum tu. Nen tang ky thuat cua Google Suggest dua tren mot kien truc don gian nhung hieu qua tich hop cong nghe kiem tra chinh ta va thuat toan PageRank danh tieng de xep hang va hien thi nhung tu khoa co tan suat xuat hien cao nhat.

Quy trinh van hanh cua dich vu nay kha truc quan khi nguoi dung chi can tuong tac voi o tim kiem de kich hoat danh sach de nghi dang tha xuong. Sau khi chon mot goi y phu hop he thong se ngay lap tuc dieu huong den trang hien thi ket qua. Mot dac diem can luu y ve mat trai nghiem la Google Suggest hien chua co nut bam truc tiep de quay tro lai giao dien tim kiem ban dau tu trang ket qua. De thuc hien dieu nay nguoi dung can su dung lenh Back tren thanh cong cu trinh duyet hoac tinh chinh truc tiep tren thanh dia chi URL. Cu the ban can xoa cac thanh phan phu tro trong duong dan tu phia ben phai cho den khi dat den tham so complete=1 tai dia chi http://www.google.com/webhp?complete=1 de duy tri trang thai hoat dong cua cong cu. Day la mot giai phap ky thuat tam thoi trong giai doan thu nghiem giup nguoi dung duy tri ket noi voi he thong goi y tu dong truoc khi Google hoan thien kha nang dieu huong khep kin cho san pham.
