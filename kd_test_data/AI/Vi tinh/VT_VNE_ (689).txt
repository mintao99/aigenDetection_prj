Microsoft dang trien khai cac dot nang cap cau hinh he thong tren Windows 2000, XP va Windows Server 2003 nham han che rui ro tu cac cuoc tan cong vao may chu IIS. Diem nhan ky thuat quan trong nhat trong dot cap nhat nay la viec vo hieu hoa thanh phan ADODB.Stream, mot cong cu ma tin tac dang loi dung de sao chep ma doc vao thiet bi nguoi dung. Theo dai dien tu Trung tam phan ung nhanh cua Microsoft, hang cung dang khan truong hoan thien cac ban va loi bo sung cho trinh duyet Internet Explorer trong thoi gian toi nham ngan chan cac hanh vi thuc thi ma lenh trai phep.

Cac cuoc tan cong gan day do nhom Hangup Team thuc hien da danh vao cac doanh nghiep chua cap nhat va loi cho IIS 5.0, cho phep hacker thay doi cau hinh server de gan ma doc vao file HTML. Lo hong bao mat nay ket hop voi khiem khuyet trong Internet Explorer tao dieu kien cho Trojan xam nhap he thong va danh cap thong tin nhay cam nhu mat khau hay tai khoan ngan hang. Mac du ADODB.Stream la mot tinh nang ActiveX hop le cho phep tuong tac voi file nhi phan thong qua giao dien web, nhung do muc do su dung thuc te khong cao va rui ro bi khai thac qua lon, Microsoft da quyet dinh ngat bo tinh nang nay de bao ve nguoi dung.

Doi voi cac he thong dac thu can duy tri ADODB.Stream, Microsoft da cung cap huong dan dieu chinh registry de tuy bien cau hinh. Dong thoi, hang cung canh bao ve cac loi scripting lien vung co the danh lua trinh duyet tai ve noi dung khong an toan. Mac du phien ban Windows XP Service Pack 2 sap toi se duoc tang cuong kha nang phong thu tong the, nguoi dung hien tai van duoc khuyen cao su dung tinh nang Windows Update de cap nhat cac ban va moi nhat, dam bao an toan cho du lieu va he thong truoc cac ky thuat tan cong tinh vi.
