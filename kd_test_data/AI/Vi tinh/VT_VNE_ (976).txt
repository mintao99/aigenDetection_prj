He thong SmartBank phien ban 3.0 do Cong ty Giai phap Phan mem FPT phat trien la mot giai phap quan tri ngan hang toan dien duoc xay dung tren nen tang ha tang ky thuat tu cac doi tac cong nghe hang dau nhu Microsoft, Oracle, IBM va HP. Giai phap nay tung dat giai Cup bac tai trien lam ICT 2004 nho kha nang toi uu hoa cac quy trinh nghiep vu va tich hop da dang cac kenh giao dich hien dai nhu ATM hay Internet Banking. Voi kien truc gom 11 phan he chuyen sau, SmartBank 3.0 cung cap he sinh thai quan ly tu so cai, tien gui, tin dung den tai tro thuong mai va dau tu. Diem nhan ky thuat cua he thong nam o kha nang xu ly du lieu tap trung, tinh tham so hoa linh hoat va co che bao mat nghiem ngat nham dam bao an toan cho cac giao dich truc tuyen moi luc moi noi.

Phan he quan ly thong tin khach hang dong vai tro hat nhan trong kien truc phan mem, noi moi thuc the duoc dinh danh bang mot ma duy nhat lien ket chat che voi cac tai khoan nghiep vu. Dieu nay cho phep ngan hang kiem soat chinh xac tinh trang tai san, du no va chu ky khach hang tren toan he thong mot cach dong bo. Ve mat ky thuat, SmartBank 3.0 van hanh tren co so du lieu MS SQL Server hoac Oracle, su dung ngon ngu lap trinh VB va tuong thich tot voi cac he dieu hanh Windows NT, 2000 hoac Unix. De trien khai, he thong yeu cau cau hinh may chu toi thieu gom chip Pentium IV 2,8 GHz va 2 GB RAM, trong khi may tram can chip Pentium IV 2 GHz cung 512 MB RAM. Hien tai, giai phap da duoc trien khai thanh cong tai hon 10 ngan hang nhu Habubank, Sacombank va mo rong sang thi truong Lao, Campuchia voi dinh huong tiep can them Myanmar hay Philippines trong tuong lai gan.
