Yahoo vua thong bao ke hoach dinh chi phan khuc phong chat do nguoi dung tu khoi tao tren nen tang cua minh nham that chat quan ly noi dung. Quyet dinh nay duoc dua ra sau khi dai truyen hinh KPRC cong bo cac phong su dieu tra ve viec lam dung tinh nang nay de trao doi cac noi dung khieu dam tre em duoi nhung tieu de gay soc. He qua truc tiep la cac doi tac quang cao lon nhu PepsiCo va State Farm Insurance da lap tuc rut cac bieu ngu quang cao khoi he thong de bao ve hinh anh thuong hieu. Dai dien PepsiCo cho biet ho khong he hay biet thuat toan chen quang cao da dua thuong hieu vao cac khong gian doc hai nay va da yeu cau go bo ngay lap tuc.

Mary Osako tu phia Yahoo xac nhan viec dong cua cac phong chat tu tao la mot phan trong lo trinh nang cap len phien ban dich vu moi. Hang cung nhac nho cong dong ve cac dieu khoan su dung nghiem cam hanh vi bat hop phap hoac vi pham thuan phong my tuc tren trang web. Trong khi cac phong chat do he thong quan ly van duy tri hoat dong, lo trinh khoi phuc tinh nang tu tao van chua duoc xac dinh cu the. Cac chuyen gia bao mat tu WiredSafety danh gia cao dong thai nay nhung nhan manh Yahoo can thiet lap cac bo loc ky thuat hieu qua hon truoc khi mo lai dich vu.

Ho so an ninh mang cho thay day khong phai lan dau Yahoo doi mat voi van de nay khi vao nam 2001, FBI tung triet pha mot duong day toi pham lon ngay tren nen tang nay voi hon 100 doi tuong bi bat giu. Hien tai, nguoi dung van duoc khuyen cao than trong khi truy cap cac nhom cong cong nhu Culture and Community do tinh trang noi dung tho tuc van ton tai. Dac biet, nguoi dung can canh giac cao do voi cac lien ket la duoc chia se trong cua so chat de tranh cac rui ro ve ma doc va bao mat thong tin ca nhan.
