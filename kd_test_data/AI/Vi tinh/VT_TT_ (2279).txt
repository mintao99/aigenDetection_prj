Thiet bi USB-TV cua Humax la mot giai phap ngoai vi hieu qua giup chuyen doi may tinh ca nhan hoac laptop thanh mot tram thu phat truyen hinh ky thuat so mat dat chuyen dung. San pham hoat dong tren dai tan VHF va UHF voi kha nang thu nhan tu 18 den 42 kenh tuy thuoc vao dieu kien ha tang song tai tung khu vuc cu the. Thiet bi su dung giao tiep USB 2.0 hoac 1.1 va lay dien nang truc tiep 5V tu cong ket noi cua may tinh ma khong can nguon phu ben ngoai. De van hanh on dinh, he thong yeu cau cau hinh toi thieu gom he dieu hanh Windows 2000 hoac XP, vi xu ly xung nhip 800MHz, bo nho RAM 128MB cung card do hoa 16MB co ho tro Microsoft DirectX 9.0.

Ve mat phan cung, Humax USB-TV tich hop ang-ten kim loai co thiet ke dang day deo tinh te nhung van dam bao do nhay thu tin hieu cao. Trong moi truong song yeu, nguoi dung co the thay the bang ang-ten rau di kem de toi uu hoa kha nang tiep nhan. Diem noi bat cua thiet bi la tinh di dong cao khi co the duy tri viec thu song on dinh ngay ca khi dang di chuyen voi van toc len toi 80km/h. Phan mem dieu khien di kem ho tro do kenh tu dong va cung cap giao dien tuong tac truc quan ngay tren man hinh may tinh.

Ben canh cac tinh nang co ban nhu chup anh man hinh hay lap trinh ghi chuong trinh, thiet bi con ho tro theo doi da kenh dong thoi thong qua che do chia o. Dac biet, phan mem chuyen dung do Cong ty Dien tu cong nghiep CDC phat trien cho phep ghi lai toan bo luong du lieu tren mot bang tan, giup nguoi dung xem lai tat ca cac kenh trong cung mot tan so phat. Kha nang chia se qua mang noi bo LAN cung la mot diem cong ky thuat dang chu y giup nhieu may tinh cung khai thac du lieu tu mot thiet bi duy nhat. Voi doanh so an tuong hon 1.000 don vi trong thang dau tien, CDC du kien se nang cap dong san pham nay bang viec tich hop them bo nho luu tru du lieu trong tuong lai gan.
