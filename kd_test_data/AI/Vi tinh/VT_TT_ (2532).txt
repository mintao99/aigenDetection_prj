Windows Messenger duoc tich hop san trong he dieu hanh Windows XP cung cap nhieu giai phap giao tiep truc tuyen chuyen sau thay vi chi dung lai o cac tinh nang chat thong thuong. De van hanh he thong, nguoi dung can thiet lap tai khoan .NET Passport bang cach truy cap vao menu File va chon muc dang ky thong qua cac buoc xac thuc email va mat khau co ban. Sau khi dang nhap thanh cong, nguoi dung co the khai thac tinh nang Remote Assistance de truyen tai tin hieu hinh anh man hinh den may tinh doi tac sau khi thuc hien cac buoc xac nhan ket noi hai chieu.

Diem dac biet cua cong cu nay con nam o giao thuc Application Sharing cho phep chia se quyen kiem soat ung dung giua cac thiet bi. Thong qua viec kich hoat che do Allow control, may tinh duoc chia se co kha nang can thiep truc tiep vao phan mem de chinh sua hoac dieu khien nhu dang thao tac tren may cuc bo. Ben canh do, tinh nang Whiteboard cung cap mot bang ve ky thuat so chung giup cac ben tuong tac do hoa thoi gian thuc va cho phep sao chep du lieu tu nhieu nguon khac nhau.

Tuy nhien hieu suat van hanh cua cac dich vu nay phu thuoc lon vao bang thong duong truyen dan den hien tuong do tre tin hieu hoac giam chat luong hinh anh trong qua trinh su dung. Du cac cong cu ho tro trong Whiteboard con o muc co ban va chua thuc su phong phu, Windows Messenger van la mot giai phap ket noi mang huu hieu cho muc dich trao doi cong viec va ho tro ky thuat tu xa tai thoi diem nay.
