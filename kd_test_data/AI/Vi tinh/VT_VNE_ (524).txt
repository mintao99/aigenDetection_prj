Du an Flashmob 1 do cac nha nghien cuu tai Dai hoc San Francisco thuc hien da danh dau mot buoc tien trong viec khai thac kien truc tinh toan phan tan tu cac thiet bi dan dung. Thay vi su dung cac he thong may chu chuyen dung dat do, nhom nghien cuu da huy dong hon 700 may tinh ca nhan va laptop tu cac tinh nguyen vien de thiet lap mot mang luoi tinh toan dien rong thong qua ket noi mang noi bo LAN. Moi thiet bi tham gia duoc cai dat mot phan mem chuyen dung nham thuc thi cac thuat toan phuc tap duoi dang cac tac vu song song lien ket chat che voi nhau.

Ve mat thong so ky thuat, du muc tieu ban dau duoc ky vong dat muc cong suat 403 Gigaflops, tuong duong 403 ty phep tinh moi giay, nhung hieu suat thuc te thu duoc chi dung lai o nguong 180 Gigaflops. Trong tong so cac thiet bi ket noi, chi co 669 may duy tri duoc trang thai lien thong on dinh de tham gia vao chu trinh tinh toan chung. Tro ngai lon nhat nam o kha nang dong bo hoa va su co phan cung cua cac nut mang khi he thong van hanh o cong suat toi da. Thuc te, cong suat dinh diem do duoc chi la ket qua hiep luc tu 256 may tinh hoat dong hieu qua nhat. Viec xac dinh cac thiet bi khong tuong thich va toi uu hoa cau hinh de chay cac trinh kiem tra hieu nang benchmark la thach thuc ky thuat cot loi ma doi ngu phai doi mat.

Mac du chua the lot vao danh sach 500 sieu may tinh manh nhat the gioi, nhung du an da chung minh kha nang toi uu hoa chi phi so voi cac he thong tri gia hang chuc trieu USD nhu Earth Simulator cua NEC. Mo hinh tinh toan dan chu nay mo ra huong di moi trong viec xu ly cac bai toan phuc tap ve mo hinh sinh hoc hay nghien cuu vu tru ma khong can den nguon ngan sach khong lo len toi 100 trieu USD cho cac sieu may tinh truyen thong.
