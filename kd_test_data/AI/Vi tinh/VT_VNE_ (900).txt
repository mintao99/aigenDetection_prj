Ninja Gaiden tren he may Xbox duoc phat trien boi Team Ninja va phat hanh boi Tecmo vao dau nam 2004 la mot buoc tien dot pha trong the loai hanh dong phieu luu. Tua game nay khong chi ke thua di san tu cac phien ban arcade va NES ma con nang tam trai nghiem thong qua mot he thong chien dau phuc tap va day thuyet phuc. Cot truyen xoay quanh hanh trinh cua Ryu Hayabusa tai de quoc Vigoor duoc dan dat qua cac doan cat canh co tinh nghe thuat cao voi ket thuc mo goi mo nhieu bi an. Diem nhan ky thuat nam o co che dieu khien truc quan va linh hoat cho phep nguoi choi thuc hien cac to hop don the lien hoan dep mat trong mot moi truong duoc thiet ke chi tiet.

He thong gameplay cua tro choi the hien su dau tu ky luong vao thuat toan tri tue nhan tao khien ke thu tro nen thong minh va nguy hiem hon bao gio het. Ryu so huu bo ky nang da dang tu khinh than chay tren tuong den du xa va chien dau duoi nuoc voi cac thao tac phan hoi tuc thi. Ben canh yeu to hanh dong toc do cao thiet ke man choi con tich hop cac cau do logic va he thong nang cap vu khi chieu thuc sau sac. Nguoi choi can thu thap tai nguyen tu chien loi pham de toi uu hoa suc manh nhan vat va mo khoa cac nang luc moi tao nen su can bang giua tinh toan chien thuat va ky nang thao tac tay cam.

Ve mat do hoa Ninja Gaiden thiet lap mot tieu chuan moi voi hieu ung hinh anh uy nuyen va moi truong game da dang tu kien truc chau Au co dien den boi canh hien dai. Su ket hop giua am thanh mang am huong Nhat Ban truyen thong va cac hieu ung tieng dong chan thuc mang lai cam giac nhu dang thuong thuc mot sieu pham dien anh tuong tac. Day thuc su la mot tac pham hoan hao ve mat ky thuat mang lai thu thach lon va su thoa man toi da cho nhung game thu dam me the loai hanh dong chuyen sau.
