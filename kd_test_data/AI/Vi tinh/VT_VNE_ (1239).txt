Buoc sang nam 2005, ha tang an ninh mang dang dung truoc nhung thach thuc mang tinh buoc ngoat khi cac phuong thuc tan cong truyen thong dan tro nen tinh vi hon. Cong nghe nhan dien virus dua tren chu ky so dang boc lo nhung han che dang ke truoc su bung no cua cac bien the ma doc moi, von da dat moc 1.300 loai chi trong mot thang cuoi nam. De doi pho, nganh bao mat dang chuyen dich manh me sang viec tich hop ky thuat phan tich hanh vi heuristics. Phuong phap nay cho phep he thong giam sat va co lap cac tap tin dua tren cac dau hieu hoat dong kha nghi, chang han nhu viec nhung bo may SMTP vao ma nguon email, thay vi chi dua vao cac mau nhan dien tinh da co san.

Song song voi do, van nan thu rac duoc du bao se chiem toi 90 phan tram luu luong email toan cau. Cac doi tuong phat tan dang ap dung ky thuat tan cong tu dien de do quet va gui tin nhan hang loat toi cac dia chi tiem nang theo thu tu bang chu cai. Mac du cac bien phap phap ly da duoc tang cuong, nhung su thieu dong bo giua cac quoc gia khien cac nhom spammer de dang di chuyen ha tang sang nhung khu vuc chua co luat dinh ro rang. Trong khi do, vien canh ve cac cuoc tan cong khung bo mang quy mo lon van chi dung lai o muc don doan. Thuc te cho thay cac bang nhom toi pham co to chuc dang tap trung nguon luc vao viec khai thac lo hong de danh cap du lieu ngan hang va the tin dung, von mang lai loi ich tai chinh truc tiep hon.

Mot xu huong cong nghe noi bat khac la mo hinh kiem soat truy cap dua tren trang thai bao mat cua thiet bi dau cuoi. Theo do, cac may chu doanh nghiep va dich vu web se thuc hien quet cau hinh an ninh cua may tinh nguoi dung truoc khi thiet lap ket noi. Neu he thong phat hien thiet bi chua duoc cap nhat ban va hoac dang nhiem ma doc, quyen truy cap se bi han che hoac tu choi hoan toan. Day duoc xem la mot buoc di quyet liet cua nganh cong nghe nham thiet lap mot tieu chuan bao mat dong nhat cho moi truong Internet trong tuong lai gan.
