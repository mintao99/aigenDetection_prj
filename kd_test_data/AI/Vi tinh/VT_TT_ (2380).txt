Giai phap phan mem moi nay duoc xay dung tren mot nen tang thuat toan chuyen dung nham tich hop cac nguon du lieu trac dia chi tiet va so do ky thuat cua cac cong trinh cao tang. He thong khong chi luu tru thong tin ve vi tri dia ly ma con phan tich cac diem tiem an nguy co tac nghen hoac su co ky thuat trong khong gian do thi. Thong qua co che thiet lap hang rao dia ly ao, cong nghe nay cho phep ngan chan hieu qua cac phuong tien bay xam nhap trai phep vao khong phan han che hoac cac khu vuc co rui ro va cham cao.

Ve mat van hanh, phan mem thuc hien quy trinh giamsat lien tuc toa do may bay bang cach xu ly cac chi so thoi gian thuc tu he thong radar mat dat va tren khong. Trong kich ban phat hien vat can hoac nguy co va cham truc dien, he thong se kich hoat giao dien canh bao tuc thi voi den tin hieu do va am thanh bao dong cuong do cao trong vong hai giay. Diem cot loi cua giai phap nay nam o kha nang can thiep tu dong, theo do he thong se tu thuc hien cac thao tac dieu huong va hieu chinh quy dao bay neu phi cong khong kip thoi phan hoi cac tin hieu canh bao khan cap.

Day duoc danh gia la mot tien bo cong nghe co tinh ung dung cao voi chi phi trien khai toi uu, dong vai tro nhu mot lop bao mat bo sung cho he thong dieu hanh khong luu. Bang cach giam thieu toi da cac vu tai nan tham khoc do va cham giua cac may bay hoac dam vao cao oc, phan mem gop phan chuan hoa quy trinh an toan hang khong toan cau. Viec ap dung rong rai cong nghe nay khong chi bao ve ha tang dan dung ma con nang cao dang ke chi so tin cay cho nganh hang khong dan dung trong ky nguyen so hien nay.
